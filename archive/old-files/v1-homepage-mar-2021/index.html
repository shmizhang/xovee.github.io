Xovee Xu (徐增)

Bio: Xovee Xu is current a third-year master student major in software engineering at the University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC), Chengdu, Sichuan, China. He received the B.S. degree in software engineering from UESTC in 2018.
He is the author of several research articles published in INFOCOM, GLOBECOM, and CSUR. His research interests include social network data mining and knowledge discovery, especially focus on information diffusion, information cascade, user behavior analysis, spatial-temporal data modeling, large-scale graph learning, and science of science in various real-world applications.
Mr. Xu has served as reviewer for refereed conferences and journals including KDD, AAAI, TKDE, TNNLS, and TON. He is a member of the IEEE and ACM. Curriculum Vitae [.pdf]
